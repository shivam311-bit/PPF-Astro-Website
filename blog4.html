Astrology Life Path Number Explained

Life path numbers are simple numerology derived from your birth date. They highlight core lessons and strengths. This guide gives the method and practical uses for planning career and personal decisions.

How to calculate quickly

Example: Birthdate 14-07-1996 → 1+4+0+7+1+9+9+6 = 37 → 3+7 = 10 → 1+0 = 1. Life path = 1.

Using numbers in daily life

A person with life path 1 benefits from leadership roles; life path 2 excels in partnership roles; life path 7 prefers research and solitude. Use the number to pick tasks, set short-term goals and choose learning paths.
