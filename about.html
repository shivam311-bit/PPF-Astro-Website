About PPF

PPF (Past | Present | Future) is an interactive astrology and prediction platform designed to give users daily insights, lucky numbers, kundli analysis, and fun future predictions. The goal is to offer a clean, modern and user-friendly experience while keeping everything fast and secure.

Who Made This?

Hi! I'm Shivam, the creator of PPF. I am currently a B.Tech CSIT student at DGIs (Dronacharya Group of Institutions) and I enjoy building modern apps, websites and games.

I created PPF as a personal project to combine design, programming, astrology, and user engagement. I believe technology should be simple, beautiful, and helpful.
