About PPF

PPF — Past, Present, Future is a modern cosmic astrology platform built to give quick, entertaining and insightful astrological guidance. We combine classic astrology concepts (kundli, nakshatra, moon sign) with fun interactive tools — palm scanning, love compatibility, daily lucky colour/number/time and lifestyle-based health pointers. Everything runs client-side and is privacy-first unless you opt to sync.

Our Mission

We aim to make astrology approachable and inclusive — no religious barriers, just helpful tools to explore personality, relationships and daily luck. Our modern theme and simple UI let users share results easily and come back daily for fresh insights.

What we provide

  • Basic Kundli & Moon sign (quick, approximate results)
  • Palm Scan (camera-based, fun estimates)
  • Love Cosmic — compatibility percentage with pie chart
  • Daily Lucky Table — color, number, and time that update every day
  • Local account support to save your history (login/register)

Transparency

Our site provides approximate results for entertainment and light guidance. For exact Vedic charts, planetary positions, and clinical advice, consult a qualified astrologer or relevant professional.
