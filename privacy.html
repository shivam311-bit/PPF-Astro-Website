Privacy Policy

Last updated:

PPF (Past, Present, Future) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

  • Personal information you provide: when you register we store username, email and password in localStorage (client-side) unless you enable server sync.
  • Local data: readings you generate (kundli, palm scans, love results) are stored locally to give you history and convenience.
  • Device & usage info: browser information and anonymous usage patterns to improve the product (client-only for now).

How we use data

  • Provide and save your readings locally.
  • Show personalized daily lucky results when you are logged in.
  • Improve the website and fix issues.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense if you enable them. These services have their own privacy policies.

Security

Accounts and history are stored in the browser (localStorage) by default. If you ask to enable server sync we will add a secure backend (Firebase / Supabase) and disclose encryption details at that time.

Your choices

  • Delete local data by clearing browser storage
  • Opt out of server sync (we will not store your data server-side without permission)
