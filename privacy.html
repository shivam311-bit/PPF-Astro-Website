Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03 December 2025

PPF ("we", "our", "the Site") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we may collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Personal information you provide: when you contact us, register, or sign up for features, we may collect name and email address. We DO NOT store user passwords as plain text on our servers or browser storage.
  • Local data: readings or results you generate (kundli, palm scans, compatibility) are stored locally in your browser by default to provide quick access. These are stored in browser storage and remain on your device unless you enable server sync.
  • Usage & device data: we may collect anonymized data about how the Site is used to improve user experience. This may include browser type, screen size, and general usage patterns.

How we use information

  • To provide the services and display your saved readings.
  • To respond to contact requests or support inquiries.
  • To analyze usage for improving the Site and fixing bugs.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information. By default, your readings and profile are stored locally in your browser. If you choose to enable server sync (optional), we will use secure, industry-standard practices (e.g., hashed passwords, TLS). We will document encryption and storage methods if server sync is enabled.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services such as analytics or advertising (Google Analytics, Google AdSense). These third parties have their own privacy policies. If you enable ads, those providers may collect information as described in their policies.

Your choices

  • You can delete local data by clearing browser storage.
  • You can opt out of server sync; we will not store data server-side without explicit permission.
  • To request data deletion or ask privacy questions, contact: stha85620@gmail.com.

By using the Site you agree to this policy. We may update the policy from time to time; changes will appear here with an updated date.
