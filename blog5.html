How Your Name Affects Your Destiny (Numerology Guide)

This friendly numerology guide shows how to reduce letters to numbers and interpret the core energies a name carries. It’s an easy tool to refine career and personal branding choices.

Convert letters to numbers

Use the standard numerology chart (A=1, B=2 ... I=9, J=1, etc.). Add letters and reduce to a single digit. Compare name number with life path to find alignment.

Practical tips

If your name number clashes strongly with your life path, consider small branding edits (pen name, business name tweaks) to improve resonance. Always test changes for 3–6 months.
