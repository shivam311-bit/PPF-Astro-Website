What Your Birth Day Tells About Your Career

From the number to the weekday, small birth date clues can point to natural strengths. This short guide helps you match day-patterns to career choices.

Numeric day insights

Days like 1,10,19 are leadership-oriented; 2,11,20 favor collaboration; 3,12 creative expression. Use these patterns as a tie-breaker when choosing between career paths.
